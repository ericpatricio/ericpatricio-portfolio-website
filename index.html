About me

Who am I?

My name is Eric Patricio, born and raised in Dominican Republic. I moved to Miami, Florida at the age of 21 years old. I came from a background in warehouse operation. Husband and father of two beautiful children, a boy and a girl.

At the end-year of 2019, I realized that I am not doing what I really like, my love and passion for tech and computers, I decided to invest in myself and learn to code through the Front-End Web Development, with different online platform as Udemy, freeCodeCamp, YouTube tutorial and MDN Web Docs. Focus in my long-term goal to be a strong full stack developer. I am currently working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but I am widening my skill set daily. The most recent library I have begun learning is React js.

When I'm not on the computer, I am with my family, playing with my kids, doing the market or a little be of a handyman around the house.
